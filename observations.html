The observations for PRIMER

PRIMER will image the two key equatorial HST CANDELS Legacy Fields (COSMOS and UDS), as shown in the figure below.

PRIMER will obtain deep imaging in 8 bands with NIRCam: F090W, F115W, F150W, F200W, F277W, F356W, F444W, and F410M, and in 2 bands with MIRI: F770W and F1800W.
